About Us

The Datalifecycle™ Company is a vendor-neutral integrator of data storage solutions.

Data is the lifeblood of the modern organization. Access to, and protection of electronic data is vital to the health of the enterprise. With the rapid commoditization of storage technologies, feature differentiation between products is now increasingly nuanced. Datalifecycle™ has the specialized experience to understand where these nuances add value – and where they only add avoidable costs to a project.

Our business, therefore, is based on our fundamental conviction that independent solutions are more viable for our clients, offering greater flexibility, better value and a superior "fit".

Our ability to consistently provide such solutions to our partners and clients is our greatest strength.

We have built strategic partnerships with specialist niche vendors as well as big brands – but we put your needs first, always striving to find the right mix of features, functionality and price that works for you.

Our business model is a combination of :
  • Direct Sales
  • OEM / Reseller Sales
  • Technical Support
  • Consultancy
  • Lease of Storage infrastructure
  • Data Disposal services
We are headquartered in Bangalore, with offices in Chennai and Hyderabad.

Mission
We aim to be the best storage solutions integrator in India, consistently delivering designed-to-fit solutions that optimally manage the data lifecycle of IT enabled organisations, without compromising on our independence.
